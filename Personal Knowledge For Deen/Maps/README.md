# Maps

This folder holds geographic references for the encyclopedia: described maps, ASCII maps, and (later) image maps of the lands where Islamic history unfolded.

## Note on Season 01

**Season 01 (Before Humanity) has no maps** — its "geography" is the unseen (*al-ghayb*): the Throne, the Kursi, the heavens. There is no physical terrain until the creation of the earth and the descent of Adam (Season 02).

## Halal-imagery rule

Maps may show terrain, cities, routes, and place-names freely. They must **never** depict the Prophets, angels, or Sahaba as figures. Use markers, labels, and symbols instead.

*Files are added as later seasons are written. Use descriptive filenames, e.g. `S5_ibrahim-journey.png`.*
