# The Master Timeline
### The Chronological Spine of All Existence

This is the backbone of the entire encyclopedia — the single thread that connects "before time" to the present. Each entry links forward to the season/episode where it is told in full. It will be extended season by season.

> ⚠️ **On dates:** For the pre-creation and earliest eras, **there are no calendar dates** — these events are *ghayb* and outside our timeline. Dates only become approximate-and-debated from the Prophets onward, and reasonably firm only with the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ (the *Seerah*). Where a date is uncertain or disputed, it is marked. The Hijri calendar (AH) begins at the Hijrah (622 CE), covered in Season 13.

- The further back, the less precise the dating — and this encyclopedia says so honestly rather than inventing certainty.
- Each era's full detail lives in its season's chapters. This file is the map; the chapters are the territory.
