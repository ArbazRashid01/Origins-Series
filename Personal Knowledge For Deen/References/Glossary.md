# Glossary of Key Terms
### Arabic & Islamic Terminology Used Throughout the Encyclopedia

Terms are grouped by theme. Each entry gives the Arabic sense, a plain definition, and where relevant the chapter where it is first explained in depth.

---

## Allah, Creed & The Unseen

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Allah** | The proper name of God — the One, the only deity worthy of worship |
| **Tawhid** | The absolute Oneness of Allah (in Lordship, Worship, and Names/Attributes) — *S1E1* |
| **Shirk** | Associating partners with Allah; the gravest sin, the opposite of tawhid |
| **Aqidah** | Creed — the body of core Islamic beliefs |
| **Al-Ghayb** | The Unseen — realities beyond the senses (Allah, angels, jinn, Hereafter) |
| **Al-Asma' al-Husna** | The Beautiful Names of Allah — *S1E1* |
| **Al-Awwal / Al-Akhir** | The First / The Last — Allah's eternality, no beginning, no end — *S1E1* |
| **Al-Ghani** | The Self-Sufficient; Allah needs nothing — *S1E1* |
| **Istiwa'** | Allah's rising/establishing over the Throne, affirmed without "how" — *S1E1* |
| **Al-'Arsh** | The Throne — the greatest created thing — *S1E1* |
| **Al-Kursi** | The Footstool — immense creation, smaller than the Throne — *S1E1* |
| **Al-Qalam** | The Pen — first commanded to write the decree — *S1E1* |
| **Al-Lawh al-Mahfuz** | The Preserved Tablet — the master record of all decree — *S1E1* |
| **Al-Qadr / Al-Qadar** | Divine Decree — the sixth pillar of faith — *S1E1* |
| **Iman bil-Ghayb** | Belief in the unseen — the first quality of the believer (2:3) |

## The Created Beings

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Mala'ikah** | The Angels — created from light, no free will — *S1E2* |
| **Jibril** | Gabriel — the angel of revelation, highest of the angels — *S1E2* |
| **Mika'il** | Michael — angel charged with rain and provision — *S1E2* |
| **Israfil** | The angel who blows the Trumpet (*As-Sur*) — *S1E2* |
| **Malak al-Mawt** | The Angel of Death (popularly "Azra'il," a non-authenticated name) — *S1E2* |
| **Hamalat al-'Arsh** | The Bearers of the Throne — *S1E2* |
| **Kiraman Katibin** | The "Noble Recorders" — the two angels who write deeds — *S1E2* |
| **Jinn** | A creation made from smokeless fire, with free will — *S1E3* |
| **Shaytan** (pl. *Shayatin*) | A rebellious, evil jinn (or evil human); a "devil" — *S1E3, E4, E5* |
| **Iblis** | The specific jinn who refused to prostrate to Adam; *the* Shaytan — *S1E4* |
| **Ar-Rajim** | "The accursed / expelled" — Iblis's earned title — *S1E4* |
| **Qarin** | The personal jinn-companion assigned to every human — *S1E3* |
| **'Ifrit** | A particularly powerful type of jinn — *S1E3* |
| **Ins / Insan** | Humankind / a human being |
| **Khalifah** | Vicegerent / successor — humanity's role on earth (2:30) — *S1E4* |

## Sources & Scholarship

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **Qur'an** | The literal, preserved word of Allah revealed to the Prophet ﷺ |
| **Surah** | A chapter of the Qur'an (114 in total) |
| **Ayah** (pl. *Ayat*) | A verse/sign of the Qur'an |
| **Sunnah** | The Prophet's ﷺ teachings, actions, and approvals |
| **Hadith** | A reported statement/action of the Prophet ﷺ |
| **Isnad / Sanad** | The chain of narrators of a hadith |
| **Matn** | The actual text/content of a hadith |
| **Sahih / Hasan / Da'if / Mawdu'** | Authentic / Good / Weak / Fabricated — see *Methodology.md* |
| **Tafsir** | Explanation/exegesis of the Qur'an |
| **Asbab al-Nuzul** | The "occasions/reasons of revelation" — context behind verses |
| **Isra'iliyyat** | Narrations from Jewish/Christian sources — labelled, not relied upon — see *Methodology.md* |
| **Athar** | Reports from the Companions or their successors |
| **Ijma'** | Scholarly consensus |
| **Qiyas** | Analogical reasoning (misused by Iblis against a direct command) — *S1E4* |

## Spiritual Concepts

| Term | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| **'Ibadah** | Worship — the purpose of creation (51:56) |
| **Ikhlas** | Sincerity — the shield Iblis admitted he cannot pierce — *S1E5* |
| **Mukhlasin / Mukhlisin** | The sincere/chosen servants, beyond Iblis's reach (38:83) — *S1E5* |
| **Kibr** | Arrogance — rejecting truth and looking down on people; Iblis's sin — *S1E4* |
| **Hasad** | Envy — the disease beneath Iblis's pride — *S1E4* |
| **Kufr** | Disbelief — including the disbelief of arrogant rejection (Iblis) — *S1E4* |
| **Tawbah** | Repentance — Adam's response, the opposite of Iblis's defiance |
| **Waswasah** | The whisper of Shaytan — his primary weapon — *S1E5* |
| **Isti'adhah** | Seeking refuge in Allah ("A'udhu billah...") — *S1E5* |
| **Ruqyah** | Lawful healing/protection by reciting Qur'an and authentic du'a — *S1E3* |
| **Tawakkul** | Reliance/trust in Allah — disarms the Shaytan (16:99) — *S1E5* |
| **Sultan** | Authority/power-to-compel — which Iblis does **not** have over believers — *S1E5* |
| **Dhikr** | Remembrance of Allah — "unties" Shaytan's knots — *S1E5* |

## Honorifics Used

| Symbol / Phrase | For whom |
|-----------------|----------|
| **ﷺ** (*sallallahu 'alayhi wa sallam*) | The Prophet Muhammad — "may Allah's peace and blessings be upon him" |
| **(peace be upon him)** / *'alayhi as-salam* | The other Prophets (Adam, Nuh, Ibrahim, Musa, Isa, etc.) |
| **(may Allah be pleased with him/her)** / *radiyallahu 'anhu/ha* | The Companions (Sahaba) |
| **(may Allah have mercy on him)** / *rahimahullah* | Deceased scholars |
