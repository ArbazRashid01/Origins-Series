# PART A — REEL SCRIPTS
---

## 🎬 S12 • E44 — "READ!" *(The First Revelation)*
**Citations:** Sahih al-Bukhari (beginning of revelation) · Qur'an 96:1–5 (first verses) · 74:1–7 (revelation resumes).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Ek aawaaz ne kaha — 'Padho!' Aur ghaar mein khade us shakhs ne kaha — 'Mujhe to padhna aata hi nahi.'"*
**🎙️ Narration:** In Hira, Jibreel said "Iqra!"; the Prophet ﷺ: "I cannot read"; squeezed thrice, then the first verses (96:1–5). He returned trembling — "cover me"; Khadijah comforted him ("Allah will never disgrace you — you keep ties, help the weak…"). Waraqah confirmed it was the same angel sent to Musa, and warned he'd be driven out. *Reflection:* even the greatest trembled — fear is human; a supportive companion who reminds you of Allah is a great mercy.
**🔄 Open loop:** Now a prophet — but announcing it would turn his city against him. → E45.

## 🎬 S12 • E45 — "THE SECRET BECOMES PUBLIC"
**Citations:** Seerah (first believers: Khadijah, Abu Bakr, Ali, Zayd) · Qur'an 26:214/15:94 (go public) · Sahih al-Bukhari (Safa) · 111 (al-Masad, on Abu Lahab).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Usne ek pahaadi par chadh kar apni qaum ko naam le kar pukaara. Aur jis sach ke liye wo use 'sachha' kehte the, usi sach ki wajah se wo palat gaye."*
**🎙️ Narration:** Three years of secret da'wah; first believers. Commanded to go public, he climbed Safa: "If I said an army was behind this hill, would you believe me?" — "Yes, you never lied." Then he warned them; Abu Lahab cursed him (Surah al-Masad). *Reflection:* people praise your truth until it costs them; truth isn't about being liked — it's about Allah's pleasure.
**🔄 Open loop:** They could insult him — but the weak would pay with their lives. → E46.

## 🎬 S12 • E46 — "THE TORTURED BELIEVERS"
**Citations:** Seerah (Bilal; the family of Yasir; Sumayyah the first martyr; Abyssinia & the Negus; the boycott; the Year of Sorrow) · Qur'an 16:106 (coercion allowance, re Ammar).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Is paigaam ke liye marne wala pehla insaan ek buddhi aurat thi — Sumayyah."*
**🎙️ Narration:** The powerless were tortured — Bilal under a rock in the sun, repeating "Ahad! Ahad!"; freed by Abu Bakr. Sumayyah (R.A.) killed by Abu Jahl — the first martyr. Some migrated to Abyssinia (the just king wept at Surah Maryam and protected them). The 3-year boycott; then the Year of Sorrow (Khadijah & Abu Talib died). *Reflection:* they endured because faith was dearer than life — what is your faith worth to you?
**🔄 Open loop:** In his darkest hour, Allah gave him a journey beyond the stars. → E47.

## 🎬 S12 • E47 — "THE NIGHT JOURNEY" *(Isra & Mi'raj — Finale)*
**Citations:** Qur'an 17:1 (al-Isra) · Sahih al-Bukhari/Muslim (the ascension; meeting the prophets; the five prayers, 50→5) · 53:13–18 (Sidrat al-Muntaha) · Seerah (Abu Bakr "As-Siddiq").
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Ek hi raat mein, wo Makkah se Jerusalem gaye — aur phir saaton aasmano se bhi ooper."*
**🎙️ Narration:** In his most grief-stricken time, the Night Journey to al-Aqsa (he led the prophets in prayer), then the ascension — meeting prophets at each heaven, reaching Sidrat al-Muntaha. The gift of the five daily prayers (originally 50, reduced to 5 with Musa's counsel; reward of 50). The mockers doubted; Abu Bakr believed instantly ("As-Siddiq"). *Reflection:* when most broken, Allah drew him closest — and the gift was *prayer*; when broken, fall into sujood.
**🔄 Open loop (→ S13):** He returned with hope — and an invitation from a city called Yathrib.
**⚠️ Guardrail:** Understood by the majority as a real, physical journey (body & soul). No depiction.

**Overview:** The terrifying first revelation, the open call that made him an enemy, the believers tortured for "Allah is One," the Year of Sorrow, and the journey beyond the heavens. Spine: *truth has a price — and Allah never abandons those who pay it.*
