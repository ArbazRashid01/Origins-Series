**3 Reels + 1 Documentary (~21 min)**
*Theme: The darker the night, the closer the dawn — the world that awaited a final Prophet.*

# PART A — REEL SCRIPTS
---

## 🎬 S11 • E41 — "A WORLD IN DARKNESS"
**Citations:** Sahih al-Bukhari (~360 idols) · Qur'an 16:58–59/81:8–9 (female infanticide condemned) · 105 (the Elephant) · 106 (Quraysh) · Seerah.
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Allah ke apne ghar — Kaaba — mein 360 buut rakhe the. Aur betiyon ko zinda dafan kar diya jaata tha."*
**🎙️ Narration:** After Isa was raised, the world darkened — "Jahiliyyat." The Kaaba (built by Ibrahim) filled with idols; the strong crushed the weak; baby girls buried alive. Abraha came with an elephant army to destroy the Kaaba — Allah sent birds (ababil) with stones; they were destroyed. *Reflection:* when things hit their worst, Allah's help is nearest — darkest before dawn.
**🔄 Open loop:** In the Year of the Elephant, an orphan was born who'd change everything. → E42.
**⚠️ Guardrail:** Present Jahiliyyah accurately (it had virtues too, but core problems were shirk + injustice). Infanticide referenced with dignity, never literal imagery.

## 🎬 S11 • E42 — "THE ORPHAN OF MAKKAH"
**Citations:** Seerah (birth in Year of the Elephant; father Abdullah died before birth; mother Amina; Abdul-Muttalib; Abu Talib) · Qur'an 93:6–8 (the orphan) · Seerah (Al-Amin; the Black Stone arbitration).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Paida hone se pehle baap ka saaya uth gaya, bachpan mein maa chali gayi. Phir bhi poora shehar use 'Al-Amin' kehta tha — sabse sachha."*
**🎙️ Narration:** Orphaned early, raised by his grandfather then uncle Abu Talib. Allah recalls it: "did He not find you an orphan and shelter you?" His reputation: Al-Amin (the trustworthy). The Black Stone dispute — he placed it on a cloth, each tribe lifting a corner, preventing bloodshed. *Reflection:* character is the greatest da'wah — people see who you are before they hear what you say.
**🔄 Open loop:** His honesty drew the attention of a businesswoman — Khadijah. → E43.
**⚠️ Guardrail:** Absolutely no depiction of the Prophet ﷺ — light/symbol only.

## 🎬 S11 • E43 — "KHADIJAH & THE CAVE" *(Finale)*
**Citations:** Seerah (Khadijah; the marriage) · Sahih al-Bukhari (her virtues; the beginning of revelation — the retreats to Hira).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Wo us par sabse pehle imaan laayi — jab baaqi sab use jhutlа rahe the. Lekin uski kahani ek tijaarat ke safar se shuru hui."*
**🎙️ Narration:** Khadijah, a noble businesswoman, hired him for trade; impressed by his honesty & character, she proposed marriage (he ~25, she ~40). She became his closest support. Troubled by his society's corruption, he retreated to the cave of Hira to reflect and worship. *Reflection:* before the greatest message, came a season of stillness and reflection — truth is often found in silence; make time alone with Allah.
**🔄 Open loop (→ S12):** One night in the cave, an angel appeared and commanded: "Read!"
**⚠️ Guardrail:** No depiction of the Prophet ﷺ or Khadijah; "Tala'a al-Badru" welcome is traditional (kept general).

## 📌 Title: "Arabia in Darkness: The World That Awaited a Prophet" · ⏱️ ~20–22 min

**Overview:** The world the final Prophet ﷺ was born into — idols, buried daughters, tribal blood — and the early life of an orphan everyone called "Al-Amin." Ends on the eve of revelation. Spine: *the darker the night, the closer the dawn.*

### 🎥 COLD OPEN
A desert wind; the dim, idol-filled Kaaba; flashes (idols; an elephant army; a desert town; a cave). *"Is sab andhere ke beech, ek yateem paida hone wala tha… ek akela insaan ek aisी bigdi duniya ko kaise badal sakta hai?"* TITLE.

### 🎙️ INTRODUCTION
Recap S10; after Isa, the message was lost. Every prophet pointed to a final one — his time was near. Timeline → "ARABIA — THE AGE OF IGNORANCE."

### CHAPTER 1 — The Age of Ignorance *(E41)* — shirk (360 idols); injustice; infanticide (81:8–9); the Year of the Elephant (105). **Why it matters:** real ignorance is of the heart — recognize your own era's "Jahiliyyat."
### CHAPTER 2 — Al-Amin *(E42)* — orphanhood & Allah's care (ad-Duha); the reputation for truthfulness; the Black Stone arbitration. **Why it matters:** character is the greatest da'wah.
### CHAPTER 3 — The Cave *(E43)* — Khadijah & the marriage; the unease with society; the retreats to Hira. **Why it matters:** truth is found in stillness — make space for it.

### 🏁 FINALE
Recap; main lesson — Allah's light arrives in the deepest darkness. → Season 12 ("Iqra!").

### 🎞️ POST-CREDIT TEASER (Season 12)
The cave; a piercing beam; a hostile city; the first believers. *"Ek aawaaz tumhe kahe 'padho,' aur tum padh hi na sako… Agle season mein — pehli wahi, aur wo qeemat jo gawaahi dene wale chukaayenge."*

### 🎨 Visual Direction
Navy/black + gold; **dusky muted gold** (a waiting world); light grows as revelation nears. Strict aniconism (Prophet ﷺ/Khadijah/family = light only; infanticide via metaphor). Signature: the dim Kaaba; the Elephant; the Black Stone; the cave of Hira.
### 🔊 Sound Direction
Vocals-only nasheed + ambient. Oppressive era-drone; ominous elephant-march → wings → silence; tender orphan theme; the stillness of the mountain.
### 📚 References Used
**Qur'an:** 105 · 106 · 81:8–9 · 16:58–59 · 93:6–8. **Hadith:** Bukhari (360 idols; beginning of revelation; Khadijah's virtues). **Seerah:** Ar-Raheeq al-Makhtum; Ibn Hisham. **Notes:** 360 idols (authentic); birth in Year of the Elephant (traditional view).
