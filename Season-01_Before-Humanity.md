# PART A — REEL SCRIPTS
---

## E1 — "BEFORE TIME EXISTED"
**Citations:** Sahih al-Bukhari ("nothing besides Him") · Qur'an 57:3 · 112 · 51:56
**HOOK:** *"Ek waqt aisa tha… jab waqt bhi nahi tha. Na zameen, na aasman. Sirf Allah."*
**NARRATION:** Aankhein band karo — kuch bhi nahi. No before or after — only Allah, al-Awwal, al-Akhir, as-Samad. By His command creation began. Your heartbeat, your existence — given by One who needed nothing.
**REFLECTION:** You were created with purpose by the One who needs nothing.
**OPEN LOOP:** Before humanity, Allah created beings of light… next episode.
**GUARDRAIL:** No speculation about "before creation." No depiction of Allah.

---

## E2 — "THE BEINGS OF LIGHT"
**Citations:** Sahih Muslim (angels from light) · Qur'an 2:285 · 66:6 · 35:1 · 82:10–12 · 40:7
**HOOK:** *"Abhi, is waqt… tumhare bilkul paas ek poori duniya maujood hai. Aur tum use dekh bhi nahi sakte."*
**NARRATION:** The unseen (al-Ghayb) is real. Angels (Malaika) — created from light, never disobey. Named: Jibreel, Mikail, Israfil, the Angel of Death. Two recording angels with you now. Some seek forgiveness for the believers.
**REFLECTION:** You are never alone — not for a single moment.
**OPEN LOOP:** But not every hidden creation was made from light — one was made from fire, and was given free will. Next episode.
**GUARDRAIL:** "Azrael" NOT firmly established — say "Angel of Death" (32:11). NO depiction of angels.

---

## E3 — "CREATURES OF FIRE"
**Citations:** Qur'an 55:15 · Sahih Muslim (jinn from smokeless fire) · 15:27 · 72:14–15 · 72:1–2 · 7:27 · 51:56
**HOOK:** *"Ek makhlooq aisi bhi hai… jo tumhe dekh sakti hai. Lekin tum unhe nahi dekh sakte."*
**NARRATION:** Jinn — from smokeless fire, before mankind. Their own world; free will like humans. Some believe, some don't. Kuch jinn ne Quran suna aur foran imaan laaye. Your choice matters — you are not forced.
**REFLECTION:** Your choices matter because you have free will.
**OPEN LOOP:** Among the jinn, one worshipped so intensely he stood among the angels — until one command changed everything. Next episode.
**GUARDRAIL:** Separate from horror/folklore. Do NOT call Iblis an angel yet.

---

## E4 — "THE ONE WHO REFUSED"
**Citations:** Qur'an 2:34 · 7:11–12 · 38:71–76 · **18:50 (Iblis was of the JINN)** · Sahih Muslim (arrogance bars Paradise)
**HOOK:** *"Isne Allah ki itni ibaadat ki, ke wo farishton ke beech ja baitha. Phir ek lamhe ne use hamesha ke liye barbaad kar diya."*
**NARRATION:** Iblis — a jinn, NOT a fallen angel (18:50 — state this on screen clearly). When Allah commanded prostration to Adam (honor, not worship), all bowed except Iblis: "I am better — fire over clay." His arrogance destroyed a lifetime of worship. Nabi ﷺ: "no one with an atom of arrogance enters Paradise."
**REFLECTION:** The danger isn't ignorance — it's knowing and refusing to submit out of ego.
**OPEN LOOP:** He didn't repent — he made a vow. Against you. Next episode.
**GUARDRAIL:** Iblis = JINN not angel (18:50) — on screen. Worship-duration = Isra'iliyyat (label it).

---

## E5 — "THE VOW AGAINST HUMANITY" *(Season Finale)*
**Citations:** Qur'an 7:14–17 · 14:22 (no power to compel) · 15:42 · 114
**HOOK:** *"Tumhare paida hone se bhi bahut pehle… kisi ne kasam khayi thi tumhe bhatkaane ki."*
**NARRATION:** Allah granted respite (part of the test). Iblis declared he'd approach from every direction. But — he has NO power to compel, only to whisper (14:22). On Judgment Day he'll say "I only invited you." The choice was always yours.
**REFLECTION:** The enemy is real but weak — he is only a voice. The decision is always yours.
**OPEN LOOP (→ Season 2):** His first target — the first human, made by Allah's own hands. His name was Adam. And that story begins now.

## Title: "Before You Existed: The Hidden Worlds" | ~25–27 min

**COLD OPEN:** Black, silence, single gold light. Flashes: light ribbons, embers, a rising ember turning shadow, dark wisps toward a human silhouette. *"Ye har kahani se… pehle ki kahani hai."* Compelling question: *"Jab kuch bhi nahi tha — tab kya tha?"* TITLE.

**INTRODUCTION:** Origins is the complete Islamic story from zero to the Prophet ﷺ. Our promise: Qur'an + sahih hadith; say "unknown" when unknown. Timeline bar rewinds to "BEFORE CREATION."

**CHAPTER 1 — Before Time Existed:** Allah's eternality (57:3; 112); as-Samad; Bukhari narration; the humility marker (speculation = off limits). Why it matters: you were created with purpose.

**CHAPTER 2 — The Beings of Light:** The unseen (2:285); angels' nature (66:6); Jibreel/Mikail/Israfil; Angel of Death (NOT "Azrael"); recording angels (82:10–12); forgiveness-seekers (40:7). Why it matters: you are never alone.

**CHAPTER 3 — Creatures of Fire:** Jinn (55:15); their world (7:27); free will (72:14); the believing jinn (72:1–2); folklore vs. truth. Why it matters: your choices matter.

**CHAPTER 4 — The First Pride:** Iblis was a JINN (18:50) — on screen; the honor-prostration; "I am better" (7:12); arrogance as the root sin; duration = Isra'iliyyat. Why it matters: knowing + refusing is more dangerous than ignorance.

**CHAPTER 5 — The Whisper & The Choice:** The respite; the vow (7:16–17); he has no power to compel (14:22; 15:42). Why it matters: the choice is always yours.

**FINALE:** Recap. Main lesson: the whole story rests on one choice — humility vs. pride. A new warm light forms (Adam). → Season 2 title card.
