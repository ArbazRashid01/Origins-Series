# ORIGINS — SEASON 10: A MIRACLE IS BORN — ZAKARIYA, MARYAM & ISA
**4 Reels + 1 Documentary (~31 min)**
*Theme: Every prophet — including Isa — came with one message: worship the One who created you.*

> ⚠️ THE MOST THEOLOGICALLY SENSITIVE SEASON. Islam honors Isa (A.S.) as a mighty **servant & messenger of Allah** — NOT divine, NOT the son of God, and (per the Qur'an) **not crucified but raised by Allah**. State respectfully, never mockingly. **Tier-A scholar review is MANDATORY.**

# PART A — REEL SCRIPTS

## 🎬 S10 • E37 — "THE PRAYER IN THE TEMPLE" *(Zakariya & Yahya)*
**Citations:** Qur'an 3:37 (Maryam's provision) · 19:4–6/3:38 (his prayer despite age) · 19:7/3:39 (Yahya) · 19:8/3:40 (how?) · 19:10/3:41 (the 3-day sign) · 19:12–14 (Yahya's righteousness).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Ek buddhа shakhs, jiski aulaad ki ummeed khatam ho chuki thi, ne ek aakhri chupke se dua maangi. Aur Allah ne use ek mojza de diya."*
**🎙️ Narration:** Old Zakariya, guardian of Maryam, found out-of-season provision with her ("it is from Allah"). Inspired, he prayed for a child despite age & a barren wife. Allah gave glad tidings of Yahya (a unique name); the sign — three days of silence. *Reflection:* he lost hope in children, never in Allah — "I was never disappointed asking You"; while there's breath, dua's door is open.
**🔄 Open loop:** If Allah gives a child to the elderly, He's about to do something never seen — a child with no father. → E38.

## 🎬 S10 • E38 — "THE CHOSEN WOMAN" *(Maryam)*
**Citations:** Qur'an 3:42 (chosen above women) · 19:17–18 (the angel; she sought refuge) · 19:19 (a pure boy) · 3:45 (a word from Allah) · 19:20/3:47 (how?) · 3:47/19:21 ("Be").
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Allah ne ek aurat ko saari duniya ki auraton mein se chuna. Aur phir use ek aisी aazmaaish di jo namumkin lagti thi."*
**🎙️ Narration:** Pure, devout Maryam — chosen and purified. The angel (Jibreel) appeared as a man; she sought Allah's refuge; he gave glad tidings of a pure boy, the Messiah Isa. "How, when no man has touched me?" — "Allah creates by His word 'Be.'" *Reflection:* Allah's decree is beyond our understanding; He gives the impossible task knowing He'll carry you through it.
**🔄 Open loop:** Unmarried and pregnant, she had to face her people alone. → E39.
**⚠️ Guardrail:** **"A word from Allah" = created by His command "Be" — NOT divine.** Parallel: Adam created by "Be" (3:59).

## 🎬 S10 • E39 — "THE BABY WHO SPOKE" *(Birth of Isa)*
**Citations:** Qur'an 19:22–23 (the palm) · 19:24–26 (the stream/dates; the vow of silence) · 19:27–28 (the accusation) · 19:29 (a cradle-child?) · 19:30–33 (his first words).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Logon ne uski paak maa par tohmat lagaayi. To us nanhe bachche ne — paalne se — apni maa ke difaa mein baat ki."*
**🎙️ Narration:** In a lonely place, childbirth pains; a voice comforted her — a stream, fresh dates; vow a fast of silence. Her people accused her; she pointed to the baby; they objected. *Climax:* baby Isa spoke — "I am the servant of Allah. He gave me Scripture and made me a prophet… and dutiful to my mother." *Reflection:* his first words declared his servanthood; and when wrongly accused and truthful, sometimes leave the matter to Allah — He clears your name.
**🔄 Open loop:** That baby would heal the blind and raise the dead — then be plotted against. → E40.
**⚠️ Guardrail:** "Sister of Harun" (19:28) = a righteous man of her time/lineage, NOT Prophet Harun.

## 🎬 S10 • E40 — "MIRACLES & THE PLOT" *(Finale)*
**Citations:** Qur'an 3:49 (miracles **by Allah's permission**) · 19:36/3:51 (his message) · 3:52 (the disciples) · 5:114 (the table) · 4:157–158 (not crucified; raised) · 4:171/5:116–117 (a servant, not divine).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Usne andhon ko aankhein deen, murdon ko zinda kiya — Allah ke hukum se. To logon ne use qatl karne ki saazish ki."*
**🎙️ Narration:** Isa's miracles — clay bird, healing, reviving the dead — each **"by Allah's permission."** His message: "Allah is my Lord and your Lord, so worship Him." The disciples (Hawariyyun); the table from heaven. They plotted to kill him; *the Qur'an states they did not kill or crucify him — Allah raised him to Himself.* He is a servant & messenger, not divine; he will testify he only called people to worship Allah. *Reflection:* we love and honor Isa deeply — and his greatest teaching is: worship the Creator alone.
**🔄 Open loop (→ S11):** Allah raised him with a promise of return; after him the world darkened again — into Jahiliyyah — awaiting a final Prophet.
**⚠️ Guardrail (critical):** Every miracle "by Allah's permission." Isa not divine, not "son of God," not crucified (4:157–158); his return is in authentic hadith (Bukhari/Muslim). Respectful toward other faiths.

## 📌 Title: "A Miracle Is Born: Zakariya, Maryam & Isa" · ⏱️ ~30–33 min *(theological precision + emotional depth)*

**Overview:** Two impossible births and the true Islamic account of Isa — his miracles by Allah's permission, his call to tawhid, and the Qur'an's clear statement that he was raised, not killed. Spine: *every prophet came with one message.*

### 🎥 COLD OPEN
A temple; a beam of light through an arch; flashes (raised hands; a light by a palm; a stream; an ascending beam). *"Isa — kaun the wo? Khuda? Khuda ke bete? Jawaab us nanhe bachche ke pehle alfaaz mein chhupа hai."* TITLE.

### 🎙️ INTRODUCTION
Recap S9; the time of Bani Israel under Roman rule; two great prophets from one family, both miraculous births.

### CHAPTER 1 — The Last Prayer *(E37)* — Zakariya's tawakkul & never-give-up dua; Maryam's provision; Yahya. **Why it matters:** Allah's ways are beyond our "practical" limits — keep praying.
### CHAPTER 2 — The Chosen *(E38)* — Maryam's rank; the annunciation; **kalimah = created by "Be" (Adam parallel, 3:59) — not divine.** **Why it matters:** Allah carries you through impossible tasks.
### CHAPTER 3 — The Cradle *(E39)* — the palm/stream; the false accusation; the cradle speech as the foundational statement: **"I am the servant of Allah."** *"Sister of Harun" clarified.* **Why it matters:** when truthful, leave your defense to Allah.
### CHAPTER 4 — By Allah's Permission *(E40)* — miracles "by Allah's permission"; his call to tawhid; the disciples; the Qur'anic account (raised, not crucified); the respectful, firm clarification of his nature (4:171; 5:116–117). **Why it matters:** servanthood is the highest honor; worship only the Creator.

### 🏁 FINALE
Recap. Main lesson: one message across all prophets — one Allah, no partner, no son. He was raised, with a promise of return. → Season 11 (Jahiliyyah).

### 🎞️ POST-CREDIT TEASER (Season 11)
A dim, idol-filled Kaaba; a single new light (an orphan). *"Ek duniya jahaan betiyaan zinda dafan ho jaayein, aur Allah ka ghar buton se bhar jaaye… ek yateem paida hoga, jise koi 'Al-Amin' kahega."*

### 🎨 Visual Direction
Navy/black + gold; **soft white + gold** (purity/miracle) — gentle, reverent. **Maximum aniconism:** Zakariya, Maryam, Isa, Jibreel = light only, never faces/figures; no nativity imagery; the raising = a beam of light. Honesty cards: kalimah = "Be"; sister of Harun; not crucified; his nature (servant & messenger).
### 🔊 Sound Direction
Vocals-only nasheed + ambient; especially soft. Hushed temple; pure annunciation tone; awe-struck hush at the cradle speech; ascending peaceful swell at the raising.
