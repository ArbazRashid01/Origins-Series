# 📖 PERSONAL KNOWLEDGE — The Full Story of Islam
### A storyteller's deep-dive into every episode of ORIGINS — for personal understanding

> This folder is **for your own knowledge and understanding of the deen** — NOT the production scripts.
> Here, nothing is skipped. Every episode is told as a flowing story, then backed with:
> - ✅ **Qur'an** (with surah:ayah)
> - ✅ **Authentic Hadith** (Sahih/Hasan, with collection)
> - ⚠️ **Weak / disputed narrations** — included, but clearly labeled as weak
> - ❌ **Isra'iliyyat** — included for awareness, clearly labeled, never as fact
> - ❓ **What is genuinely unknown** — stated honestly

---

## How to read this
Each season has its own folder. Inside, each episode has its own file, told in this order:
1. **The Story** (storyteller narration — flowing and complete)
2. **The Qur'an Says** (every relevant ayah)
3. **The Authentic Hadith** (with grading)
4. **The Historical Context** (the world, the people, the timeline)
5. **Weak & Disputed Narrations** (labeled — for awareness)
6. **What We Don't Know** (honest gaps)
7. **The Lessons** (what it means for us)
