# PART A — REEL SCRIPTS
---

## 🎬 S3 • E10 — "THE MAN WHO PREACHED FOR 950 YEARS"
**Citations:** Qur'an 71:23 + Sahih al-Bukhari (the 5 idols — Ibn Abbas) · 71:5–9 (calling night & day) · 29:14 (950 years) · 71:6–7 (mockery) · 11:40 (few believed) · Bukhari/Muslim (Nuh first messenger turned to).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Tasavvur karo — tum ek baat 950 saal samjhaao. Aur phir bhi ginti ke chand log hi sunein."*
**🎙️ Narration:** How shirk began — 5 righteous men honored, then their statues worshipped over generations (71:23 + Bukhari). Nuh sent to call them back; he tried day & night, openly & secretly. *Climax:* they mocked, put fingers in ears, turned away; 950 years, only a few believed — hence Nuh is the first messenger people turn to on Judgment Day. *Reflection:* success isn't numbers — your job is sincere effort; guidance is Allah's.
**🔄 Open loop:** Allah commanded an absurd thing — build a giant ship on dry land. → E11.

## 🎬 S3 • E11 — "THE SHIP IN THE DESERT"
**Citations:** Qur'an 11:37 (build under Our watch) · 11:38–39 (mockery + reply) · 11:40 / 23:27 (load pairs + family + believers; the oven sign) · 11:40 (few believed).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Ek aadmi ne wahaan kashti banani shuru ki — jahaan na nadi, na samandar, ek boond paani nahi."*
**🎙️ Narration:** Allah commanded the Ark; chiefs ridiculed him; Nuh: "laugh now — you'll know who gets the disgracing punishment." Command to load pairs + believers. *Climax:* the sign came — water burst even from the oven (tannur); the rain began; the "madness" was the only salvation. *Reflection:* obeying Allah can look foolish — but the one who trusts and acts is saved.
**🔄 Open loop:** As the ark floated, Nuh called everyone — even his own son — who turned away. → E12.
**⚠️ Guardrail:** Ark dimensions = Isra'iliyyat (avoid). Tannur sign = Qur'anic.

## 🎬 S3 • E12 — "THE FLOOD & THE SON WHO REFUSED" *(Finale)*
**Citations:** Qur'an 11:42 (the call) · 11:43 (refusal + wave) · 11:45 (Nuh's plea) · 11:46 ("he is not of your family") · 11:44 (Mount Judi) · 66:10 (Nuh's wife disbelieved).
**🎣 HOOK:** *"Allah ke ek Nabi ne apne sage bête ko apni aankhon ke saamne doobte dekha — aur use bachaa nahi paaye."*
**🎙️ Narration:** Waves like mountains; Nuh called his son — "embark, don't be with the disbelievers." The son: "I'll climb a mountain." Nuh: "no protector today except by Allah's mercy." A wave separated them; he drowned. *Climax:* Nuh's grief — "my son is of my family"; Allah: "He is NOT of your family; his deeds were not righteous." Faith, not blood, saves — even Nuh's wife disbelieved. *Reflection:* family/name won't save you; only your own faith and deeds.
**🔄 Open loop (→ S4):** The world restarted, but arrogance returned — a mighty nation saying "who is stronger than us?"
**⚠️ Guardrail:** Dialogue all Qur'anic; "global vs regional flood" = a scholarly difference (don't assert).

# PART B — YOUTUBE DOCUMENTARY

## 📌 Title: "Nuh: 950 Years & The Flood" · ⏱️ ~19–21 min *(focused arc, no filler)*

**Overview:** The longest act of patience in history — relentless da'wah, an absurd-seeming command, a flood that ended a world (including the prophet's own son). Spine: *salvation is never inherited.*

### 🎥 COLD OPEN
A lone calling voice in emptiness; flashes (5 lights → idols; ark on dry land; bursting water; a fading light across the flood). Question: *"Agar tumhara apna bête doob raha ho, aur tum Allah ke Nabi ho — kya tum use bachaa sakte ho? Ya kuch cheezein kisi ke haath mein nahi?"* TITLE.

### 🎙️ INTRODUCTION
Recap S1–S2; humanity drifts into idolatry after Adam. Timeline → "GENERATIONS AFTER ADAM."

### CHAPTER 1 — How the World Forgot *(E10)* — the authentic origin of shirk (the 5 idols, 71:23 + Ibn Abbas/Bukhari); Nuh's relentless, hope-based da'wah (71:10–12); psychology of rejection; 950 years (29:14). **Why it matters:** your job is effort; changing hearts is Allah's.
### CHAPTER 2 — Faith That Looked Like Madness *(E11)* — obedience defying worldly logic; ridicule; the gathering of pairs/believers as mercy; the tannur sign; *Ark build unknown.* **Why it matters:** truth is mocked before it's vindicated.
### CHAPTER 3 — Faith Over Blood *(E12)* — flood as judgment + mercy; the son's refusal; "he is not of your family"; *flood global-vs-regional = scholarly difference*; Mount Judi (11:44). **Why it matters:** each soul stands on its own faith — no one is saved by another's righteousness.

### 🏁 FINALE
Recap; main lesson (personal accountability). The greened earth; arrogance returns (towering pillars). → Season 4 card.

### 🎞️ POST-CREDIT TEASER (Season 4)
Rising wind, stone pillars, a sandstorm. *"Ek qaum itni mazboot ke unhone Allah ke azaab ko mazaaq mein challenge kiya… Agle season mein — do nishaaniyaan: ek aandhi, aur ek oonti."*

### 🎨 Visual Direction
Navy/black + gold; **stormy teal** accent. Signature: 5 lights → idols; ship on dry desert; the wave swallowing the son's light. Aniconism (drowning implied). Honesty cards: Ark build / flood scope.
### 🔊 Sound Direction
Vocals-only nasheed + ambient. Patient pulse through the centuries; flood ambience → gut-punch silence as the son's light goes out → calm at Judi.
