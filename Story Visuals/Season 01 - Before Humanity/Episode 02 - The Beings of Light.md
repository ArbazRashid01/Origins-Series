# Story Visuals · S01E02
# The Beings of Light (The Angels)
### Director's Treatment

> Pairs with: `Personal Knowledge For Deen/Season 01 - Before Humanity/Episode 02 - The Beings of Light.md`

---

## 1. Story Analysis

- **Type:** *Ghayb* / Islamic creed, with an "introducing the cast" documentary feel.
- **Core emotion:** **Reverent wonder + immensity.** The sense of a vast, ordered, luminous host beyond imagining — and comfort (these beings surround and record us).
- **Purpose taught:** Angels — made of light, no free will, countless, ranked; the named angels and their roles; their relationship to us.
- **Trap to avoid:** Do NOT draw winged human figures. Angels = **light, radiance, scale, presence** — never a face.

---

## 2. Cinematic Identity

Where Ep01 was *void*, Ep02 **fills the void with light.** The signature is **luminous multitude and radiant geometry.**

- **Palette:** Cool whites and silvers, with shafts of pure radiance; the gold of revelation reserved for Jibril.
- **Lighting:** Volumetric light beams, lens-bloom, particles of light like dust in a cathedral.
- **Camera:** Soaring, awed tilts upward; reveals of overwhelming numbers.
- **Pace:** Stately, but more "alive" than Ep01 — light now *moves.*
- **Motif:** **Wings of light** (abstract — beams, not feathered figures); endless rows.

---

## 3. Sound & Music Design

- A vast, breathing choral-like pad (wordless, non-vocal-melody to stay reverent) suggesting a tireless multitude in worship.
- Soft, continuous "rush" like a distant ocean = the ceaseless *tasbih* (glorification).
- Jibril's beat: a deeper, awe-struck swell.

LIGHT, SYM, MACRO, MG, CALLIG, ENV, SILH — strong mix. The "roster" infographic (sc.7) and correction cards (sc.12) give documentary texture; the modern-shoulder shot (sc.10) lands the emotional/personal beat. Never a single feathered figure. ✅
