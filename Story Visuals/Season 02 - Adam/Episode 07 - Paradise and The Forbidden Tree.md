# Story Visuals · S02E07
# Paradise & The Forbidden Tree
### Director's Treatment

> Pairs with: `Personal Knowledge For Deen/Season 02 - Adam/Episode 07 - Paradise and The Forbidden Tree.md`

---

## 1. Story Analysis

- **Type:** Islamic narration + the **first temptation** — psychological/spiritual drama.
- **Core emotion:** **Idyllic peace, then creeping unease.** Paradise's serenity disturbed by the whisper. Tenderness (Hawwa as companion), then the chill of the deception.
- **Purpose taught:** Hawwa created as companion; the Garden of ease with one prohibition; Iblis's whisper and false oath; the fall; the Qur'an's even-handed account (no scapegoating, no original sin).
- **Trap to avoid:** **Adam and Hawwa never shown.** No bodies, no "Eve and the apple" trope, no nakedness. The tree is unnamed/abstract. Iblis = shadow/whisper. Keep dignity; the lesson is the *whisper*, not the scene.

---

## 2. Cinematic Identity

The signature is **a luminous garden of light, with one shadow creeping in.** Beauty that holds a held breath.

- **Palette:** Lush emerald greens, soft gold, crystal water, blossom-light — the most *beautiful* palette so far — invaded by the cold blue-shadow of the whisper.
- **Lighting:** Soft, dappled paradise light; the shadow brings a subtle desaturation.
- **Camera:** Floating, serene drifts through the garden; tightens as the whisper enters.
- **Pace:** Slow and blissful, then a quickening unease.
- **Motif:** **The single tree** (abstract, glowing, set apart); falling leaves at the fall.

---

## 3. Sound & Music Design

- Paradise: gentle water, birdsong, a warm pad of contentment.
- The whisper: the *waswasah* texture from Ep05 creeping into the stereo edges; the false oath "by Allah" as a deceptively sweet, then sour tone.
- The fall: the warm pad falters; a single leaf-fall sound; silence of shame.

## 5. Visual Variety Check

ENV, NAT, SYM, LIGHT, MACRO, SLOW, CALLIG, MG — beautiful range. The garden is the series' most lush environment; the fall is rendered entirely by **symbol (dimming fruit, falling leaves)** — no bodies, no apple cliché, full dignity. The even-handed/no-original-sin graphics (sc.10) carry the key theology. Adam & Hawwa = two lights throughout. ✅
